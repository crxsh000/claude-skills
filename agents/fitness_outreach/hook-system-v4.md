## 2. The 7 Emotional Pain Points

### E1: FRUSTRATION
**"I'm working so hard but nothing's closing"**

**What to look for:**
- High engagement but low testimonials/client wins
- "DM me" CTA but comments show no conversions
- Active posting, consistent effort, but stagnant growth
- Responds to comments but convos don't go anywhere

**Hook angle:** Your effort isn't the problem. Your backend is.

**Disqualifiers:**
- Already has a sales team
- Low engagement overall (not effort issue)
- Brand new account (<6 months)

---

### E2: OVERWHELM
**"I can't keep up with the demand"**

**What to look for:**
- High follower count (10K+), high engagement
- Unanswered comments or slow replies
- Comments like "I DM'd you" or "Did you see my message?"
- Multiple offers, launches, content streams

**Hook angle:** The demand is there. You just can't catch it all.

**Disqualifiers:**
- Already has VA or team handling DMs
- Low engagement (not overwhelm, just small)
- Rarely posts

---

### E3: GUILT
**"I'm leaving leads on read and things are slipping through"**

**What to look for:**
- Visible demand but gaps in responses
- Story views high but no CTA follow-through
- "DM me" posts but quiet inbox references
- Apology posts ("sorry for late reply")

**Hook angle:** Every ignored DM is money walking away.

**Disqualifiers:**
- Responds to everything promptly
- No visible demand/engagement
- Has automated responses already

---

### E4: EXHAUSTION
**"I can't step away without income stopping"**

**What to look for:**
- Solo coach, no team mentioned
- "Finally took a day off" content
- Burnout-adjacent posts
- Everything runs through them (content, DMs, calls, coaching)

**Hook angle:** You've built a job, not a business. Let's fix that.

**Disqualifiers:**
- Has team members visible
- Passive income products as main offer
- Recently hired help

---

### E5: FEAR
**"If I stop hustling, I stop earning"**

**What to look for:**
- Constant posting, never breaks
- No mention of systems or automation
- Personal brand = entire business
- "Grind" or "hustle" language

**Hook angle:** What happens to your income when you take a week off?

**Disqualifiers:**
- Has recurring revenue model
- Recently took vacation (and survived)
- Team visible in content

---

### E6: RESENTMENT
**"I became a coach to coach, not to do sales and admin"**

**What to look for:**
- Passion for coaching visible in content
- Frustration with "business side" mentioned
- Time spent on non-coaching tasks visible
- "I just want to help people" energy

**Hook angle:** You should be coaching. I'll handle the rest.

**Disqualifiers:**
- Enjoys the sales process
- Has sales background
- Already delegated admin

---

### E7: ANXIETY
**"I don't know if next month will be good"**

**What to look for:**
- Launch-based model (feast or famine)
- "Spots open" posts followed by silence
- Inconsistent posting frequency
- Promo pushes then quiet periods

**Hook angle:** Predictable pipeline beats hoping for the best.

**Disqualifiers:**
- Consistent client flow visible
- Waitlist mentioned
- Evergreen funnel in place

### Thresholds

| Metric | Target | Kill Threshold |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Reply Rate | >5% | <2% after 50 sends |
| Reply-to-Call | >20% | <10% after 20 replies |
| Block Rate | <1% | >3% = kill immediately |

### Hook Status Definitions
- **Testing:** Under 50 sends, gathering data
- **Promising:** >5% reply rate, continue scaling
- **Scale:** >7% reply rate, high volume
- **Pause:** 2-5% reply rate, needs refinement
- **Kill:** <2% reply rate OR >3% block rate

### Weekly Review Questions
1. Which 3 hooks have highest reply rate?
2. Which emotion category performs best overall?
3. Any hooks hitting block threshold?
4. What patterns in positive replies?
5. What objections coming up repeatedly?
