# 1. TARGETING CRITERIA

## The Sweet Spot

| Factor | Too Small | Sweet Spot | Too Big |
|--------|-----------|------------|---------|
| **Followers** | <2K | 5K - 50K | 100K+ |
| **Engagement** | <50 likes | 100-500 likes | Has team already |
| **Business Stage** | Just started | 1-3 years in | Agency/multiple coaches |
| **Revenue** | <$3K/month | $5K-$30K/month | $50K+/month |
| **Team** | None needed yet | Solo or 1 VA | Full sales team |

---

## Green Flags (Target These)

| Signal | Where to Find | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| **"DM me"** in posts/bio | Bio, captions, stories | They're driving leads to inbox |
| **High engagement, low testimonials** | Feed vs. highlights | Demand exists, conversions don't |
| **Posting consistently** | Feed | They're doing the work, frontend is on |
| **5K-50K followers** | Profile | Big enough to have volume |
| **Solo coach** | Bio, content | No team = they're the bottleneck |
| **"Link in bio" to coaching offer** | Bio | Active offer, taking clients |
| **Unanswered comments** | Comment sections | Can't keep up with demand |
| **"Sorry for late reply"** | Stories, DMs | Leads are slipping |
| **Transformation posts** | Feed | Getting results, has proof |
| **1-on-1 coaching offer** | Bio, highlights | Revenue model that needs calls |
| **Online/remote coaching** | Bio | Not tied to gym, scalable |

---

## Red Flags (Skip These)

| Signal | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **<2K followers** | Not enough demand yet |
| **"Hiring" or "My team"** | Already solved the problem |
| **Agency or gym owner** | Different business model |
| **No clear offer** | Not monetized yet |
| **Course/membership only** | Doesn't need calls booked |
| **Brand new account (<6 months)** | Still building, not ready |
| **No engagement** | No demand to convert |
| **"DM for collab"** | Influencer, not coach |
| **Sells supplements/MLM** | Wrong business model |
| **Posts 1x per month** | Not active, won't respond |

## Qualification Questions

### Q1: SURFACE THE PROBLEM

*Get them to name the bottleneck*

| Option | Question |
|--------|----------|
| 1 | Where are most of your leads dying right now — DMs, follow-up, or closing? |
| 2 | What's the biggest thing stopping you from booking more calls? |
| 3 | If you could fix one thing in your sales process tomorrow, what would it be? |

---

### Q2: QUANTIFY THE PAIN

*Make the problem feel expensive*

| Option | Question |
|--------|----------|
| 1 | How many leads would you say slip through per week? |
| 2 | What's that costing you per month roughly? |
| 3 | How many "I'm interested" replies actually turn into calls? |
| 4 | How many DMs are sitting unread right now? |

---

### Q3: TRIGGER GUILT

*Make them admit they know it's a problem*

| Option | Question |
|--------|----------|
| 1 | How long has that been going on? |
| 2 | When did you first realize this was the leak? |
| 3 | How many times have you told yourself you'd fix this? |
| 4 | What's stopped you from solving this until now? |

---

### Q4: URGENCY

*What happens if nothing changes?*

| Option | Question |
|--------|----------|
| 1 | What happens if this stays broken another 6 months? |
| 2 | How much longer can you afford to leave money on the table? |
| 3 | What does your income look like if nothing changes? |
| 4 | At what point does this become an emergency? |

# 8. LOOM VIDEO TALKING POINTS

## Frame

| What It's NOT | What It IS |
|---------------|------------|
| "You have a problem" | "Let me show you something" |
| Calling out their gaps | Teaching a process |
| Criticism | Value |
| Selling | Demonstrating |

---

## Opening (15-30 sec)

- Made this specifically for you
- Wanted to show you a process that's working right now
- You can implement this yourself or not — either way, you'll get value from this

---

## Section 1: The Framework (60-90 sec)

**Talking points:**

- The coaches booking 10+ calls/week all have one thing in common — a backend system
- Most coaches focus on content (frontend) but ignore what happens after someone DMs (backend)
- The backend is: **DM → Qualify → Follow-up → Book**
- Each step has a specific purpose and structure
- Miss one step, leads leak out

**Show expertise by:**
- Naming the steps like you've done this 100 times
- Speaking in frameworks, not opinions
- Referencing what "top coaches" do

---

## Section 2: The Process — Step by Step (90-120 sec)

**Step 1: DM Response**
- Speed matters — first 30 minutes is critical
- Use pattern interrupts, not "hey how can I help"
- The first reply sets the tone for the whole conversation

**Step 2: Qualification**
- 2-3 questions max
- Goal: get them to admit the pain out loud
- Qualify on fit, urgency, and budget without asking "what's your budget"
- The right questions make them sell themselves

**Step 3: Follow-Up**
- Most leads don't convert on first touch — follow-up is where money lives
- 4-touch sequence, every other day
- Each touch has a different energy: soft → direct → urgency → exit

**Step 4: Booking**
- Assumptive close — don't ask "would you like to book", send the link
- Remove friction — one link, clear CTA
- Confirm and remind — reduce no-shows

---

## Section 3: Why This Works (30-45 sec)

**Talking points:**

- Every step increases conversion rate
- Fast reply = 3x more likely to convert
- Proper qualification = fewer tire-kickers, better calls
- Follow-up = captures 40-60% of leads that would've gone cold
- It's not about working harder, it's about plugging the leaks

---

## Section 4: How I Do This for Coaches (30-45 sec)

**Talking points:**

- I run this entire system for coaches
- They focus on content and coaching
- I handle DMs, qualification, follow-up, booking
- 10 calls free to start — you see the quality first
- Rev share after — I only win when you win

---

## Closing (15-30 sec)

- That's the system — you can run it yourself or I can run it for you
- If you want to see how it looks for your setup specifically, let's hop on a quick call
- Dropping calendar link after this
- Either way, hope this was valuable
