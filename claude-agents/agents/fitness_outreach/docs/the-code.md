## 0. THE ICP - WHO WE'RE TALKING TO

> **RULE:** Before writing ANY message, making ANY decision, or changing ANYTHING in this document — reference this ICP. Every word we write is for THIS person.

**Full ICP Document:** `icp-detailed-28-buckets.md` (1,900+ lines, 28 psychological buckets)

---

### ICP SNAPSHOT

**Who:** Instagram-based online fitness coach making $2k-$12k/month

**Age:** 26-38 (sweet spot: 28-34)

**Followers:** 2k-50k on Instagram

**Business Model:** 1-on-1 coaching, maybe experimenting with groups

---

### THE 5 CORE TRUTHS (Memorize These)

1. **They've been burned.** Spent $1,500-$5,000 on agencies, ads, or courses that flopped. They're skeptical of EVERYONE who DMs them.

2. **They're stuck in feast-or-famine.** $8k one month, $3k the next. No predictable system. Every month starts at $0.

3. **They're exhausted.** 50-60 hours/week. Content creation takes 15 hours. DM outreach takes 2+ hours. Coaching takes another 15-20. And they're still not hitting $10k.

4. **They secretly doubt themselves.** "Am I actually good enough?" "Did I start too late?" "Is everyone else just better at this than me?"

5. **They want predictability, not more leads.** They don't need 100 leads. They need 4-6 QUALIFIED calls/week that actually show up and buy.

---

### THEIR INTERNAL MONOLOGUE (Use Their Words)

**At 6am checking their phone:**
- "Did anyone book overnight?"
- "Am I shadowbanned again?"
- "That reel I spent 3 hours on got 47 views."

**After a bad month:**
- "Maybe I should just get a job."
- "Everyone else seems to be growing. Why not me?"
- "I can't keep doing this for $4k/month."

**When they see your DM:**
- "Great, another agency bro."
- "If this says 'I help fitness coaches' I'm deleting it."
- "Probably wants me to buy something."

---

### WHAT MAKES THEM OPEN A DM

| Works | Doesn't Work |
|-------|--------------|
| Acknowledging their skepticism | Generic compliments |
| Specific pain they actually feel | "I noticed your profile" |
| Curiosity without a pitch | Immediate value prop |
| Sounding like a peer, not a salesperson | Agency-speak |
| Short (1-3 sentences) | Long paragraphs |

---

For deep research, reference `icp-detailed-28-buckets.md`:

1. **Trauma** - Failed ads, burned by agencies, course that didn't work

## 1. SIMPLIFIED OUTREACH PLAN

**Goal:** 200 prospects/day in 4 hours (or 100 in 2 hours)

---

### TRACK A: No Warmup (Speed Mode)

**Time per prospect:** ~45 seconds
**Daily capacity:** 200 in 2-3 hours

| Step | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| 1 | Find prospect | 10 sec |
| 2 | Quick qualify (bio, recent post, followers) | 15 sec |
| 3 | Send DM (use variants, minimal personalization) | 20 sec |
| 4 | Next | — |

**No follow, no likes, no comments.** Pure cold DM.

---

### TRACK B: Light Warmup (Hybrid Mode)

**Time per prospect:** ~90 seconds
**Daily capacity:** 100 in 2.5 hours

| Step | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| 1 | Find prospect | 10 sec |
| 2 | Quick qualify (bio, recent post, followers) | 15 sec |
| 3 | Follow + like 1 recent post | 15 sec |
| 4 | Send DM | 20 sec |
| 5 | Next | — |

**Lighter touch.** Follow + 1 like only. Skip reels and comments.

---

### A/B Test Structure

Run each track for 1 week (500 prospects each), then compare:

| Metric | Track A (No Warmup) | Track B (Light Warmup) |
|--------|---------------------|------------------------|
| Prospects/day | 100 | 100 |
| Time spent | ~1.5 hrs | ~2.5 hrs |
| Reply rate | Track this | Track this |
| Calls booked | Track this | Track this |

**Winner = higher reply rate per hour spent**

---

### Daily Execution Checklist

**Morning Block (2 hours):**
- [ ] Open prospect source (hashtags, competitor followers, etc.)
- [ ] Run through 100 prospects using Track A or B
- [ ] Log results in your tracking system

**Afternoon Block (30 min):**
- [ ] Check for replies
- [ ] Respond to replies (move to qualification)
- [ ] Update tracking

**Weekly Review (Friday, 30 min):**
- [ ] Compare Track A vs Track B metrics
- [ ] Double down on winner
- [ ] Adjust DM variants based on reply rates

## 4. OBJECTION HANDLING SCRIPTS

Copy-paste responses for common objections.

---

### "How much does it cost?"

```
Depends on what you need, but I don't do retainers.

Let's hop on a call and I'll show you exactly what it looks like.

If it's not a fit, I'll tell you.
```

---

### "I've tried agencies before and got burned."

```
Yeah, I hear that literally every week.

That's exactly why I don't run an agency.

I'm not doing ads or setters or any of that.

Happy to show you what I mean on a call if you're curious.
```

---

### "I need to think about it."

```
Totally fair.

What specifically do you need to think through?
Timing? Budget? Whether it'll work for you?

(Might be able to answer it now and save you some time)
```

---

### "I don't have time right now."

```
Then you definitely don't have time to keep chasing flaky leads 😅

The whole point is to give you time back.

But yeah, if it's genuinely bad timing, hit me up when it clears.
```

---

### "Can you just tell me what you do?"

```
Fair ask.

Short version: I help you install a system that books 4-6 qualified calls/week
without ads, without hoping posts go viral, and without hiring setters.

If you want to see how it actually works, I can show you on a 15-min call.

Worth a look?
```

---

### "I'm already working with someone."

```
Cool — how's that going?
```

If good → "Nice, keep at it."
If bad → "Yeah, that's what I hear a lot. Want to see a different approach?"

---

### "I'll get back to you."

```
No pressure.

If I don't hear from you, I'll assume it's not a fit.

Good luck with everything 🤝
```

## 5. QUALIFICATION CRITERIA

### Quick Qualify (15 seconds)

**GREEN FLAGS (DM them):**
- 2k-50k followers
- Fitness/coaching content
- Link in bio to coaching offer
- Posts regularly (last 7 days)
- Transformation posts or client results

**RED FLAGS (Skip):**
- Under 1k followers (too early)
- Over 50k followers (different pain)
- No coaching offer visible
- Supplement seller / affiliate only
- Inactive (no posts in 2+ weeks)
- Celebrity trainer vibes

---

### Deep Qualify (After They Reply)

**Use this message after they respond:**

```
[Acknowledge their reply]

Quick context so I'm not wasting your time:

I work with coaches in the $2k-$12k/mo range
who are sick of unpredictable pipelines and tire-kicker leads.

That you or am I off base?
```

**GREEN FLAGS (Move Forward):**
- Mentions specific revenue ($4k, $8k, etc.)
- Clear pain ("I book 2 calls one week, zero the next")
- Action-oriented ("What do you recommend?")
- Urgency ("I need to fix this ASAP")

**RED FLAGS (Disqualify):**
- "I'm just starting out"
- "I don't have clients yet"
- "I'm doing this part-time"
- Asking for free advice
- Vague answers

**Disqualification script:**
```
Appreciate the honesty! Doesn't sound like the right fit right now.

If you hit [X milestone] and want to chat, hit me up.
```

---

### Call Transition (After Qualified)

```
Cool. So here's the deal.

I don't do long contracts or agency BS.

I help coaches install a system that gets you 4-6 qualified calls/week
without gambling on viral posts or burning cash on ads.

If you want to see how it works, I can walk you through it on a quick call.

15 min. No pitch. Just show you the system and you decide if it makes sense.

Worth a look?
```

## BACK POCKET: Buyer Journey Map

### The 7 Stages

1. **Problem Unaware** → Help them see the real issue
2. **Problem Aware** → Validate pain, introduce solution category
3. **Solution Aware (Skeptical)** → Differentiate, build trust
4. **Evaluation** → Answer objections, show proof
5. **Decision** → Remove friction, create urgency
6. **Purchase** → Reassure, onboard, quick win
7. **Post-Purchase** → Validate, retain, referral

### Timeline

- Problem Unaware → Problem Aware: 1-3 months
- Problem Aware → Solution Aware: 1-4 weeks
- Solution Aware → Evaluation: 3-14 days
- Evaluation → Decision: 2-7 days
- Decision → Purchase: Same day to 48 hours
- **Total: 2-6 months** (accelerated by pain)
