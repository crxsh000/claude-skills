## SHEET 4: FOLLOW-UP TRACKER

Track which stage each prospect is at:

| Handle | Opener Sent | Day 3 FU Due | Day 3 Sent? | Day 7 FU Due | Day 7 Sent? | Status |
|--------|-------------|--------------|-------------|--------------|-------------|--------|
| @coach1 | 2/3 | 2/6 | Yes | 2/10 | No | Waiting |
| @coach2 | 2/3 | 2/6 | Yes | 2/10 | — | Replied |
| @coach3 | 2/4 | 2/7 | No | — | — | Pending FU |

**Status Options:**
- **Waiting** = Sent opener, no reply yet
- **FU1 Sent** = Day 3 follow-up sent
- **FU2 Sent** = Day 7 follow-up sent (final)
- **Replied** = They responded
- **Qualified** = Replied + fits ICP
- **Call Booked** = Discovery call scheduled
- **Dead** = Negative reply or unqualified

## TRACKING WORKFLOW

### When You Send a DM:
1. Add row to Sheet 1 (DM Log)
2. Fill: Date, Handle, Variant, Followers
3. Leave rest blank for now

### When You Get a Reply:
1. Find their row in Sheet 1
2. Update: Reply = Yes, Reply Date, Sentiment
3. If positive, update Qualified and continue convo

### Day 3 Follow-Up Process:
1. Filter Sheet 1 for: Date = 3 days ago, Reply = No
2. Send Day 3 follow-up to each
3. Update Day 3 FU column with today's date

### Day 7 Follow-Up Process:
1. Filter Sheet 1 for: Day 3 FU = 4 days ago, Reply = No
2. Send Day 7 follow-up (final ping)
3. Update Day 7 FU column with today's date

### End of Week:
1. Update Sheet 3 (Variant Performance) with totals
2. Calculate reply rates
3. Identify winner
